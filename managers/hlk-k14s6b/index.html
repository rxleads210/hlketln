FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a crypto loan?
A crypto loan allows you to borrow funds in USDT quickly and conveniently. It's a fast way to access liquidity without going through traditional banking procedures.
How does a crypto loan work?
You submit a request, choose the loan terms, and receive USDT once your application is approved. The process is simple and designed to be completed without unnecessary delays.
How fast can I get a loan?
Loan processing typically takes from a few minutes up to 1–2 days, depending on the request and verification requirements.
Do I need KYC verification?
Heleket offers flexible options depending on the loan type. In some cases, minimal verification is required to speed up the process.
In what currency will I receive the loan?
All loans are issued in USDT, providing a stable and widely accepted digital asset that can be used across exchanges and wallets.
What are the interest rates and fees?
Interest rates and fees depend on the loan amount and duration. Heleket provides transparent conditions so you always know what to expect before confirming your loan.
What happens if the crypto market drops?
Market risks are the responsibility of the client. It's important to consider market volatility when taking a loan and manage your funds accordingly.
Is it safe to use Heleket?
Heleket implements modern security measures to ensure safe transactions and protect user data. The platform is designed with a focus on reliability and transparency.
How do I repay my loan?
You can repay your loan at any time according to the agreed terms. Once repayment is completed, your loan is considered closed.
Why choose Heleket for crypto loans?
Heleket offers a fast and straightforward way to access USDT without the complexity of traditional financial systems. The platform focuses on speed, simplicity, and user convenience.
